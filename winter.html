This meme is beautiful. It's all about embracing diversity. This woman in the photo is very beautiful however she is not what the people are looking for. Think about what Shrek is about, embracing our differences and choosing emotional beauty over physical beauty. The people want Shrek not beauty standards. This meme touches the heart and says it's okay to be different. Our differences is what makes each and every person so beautiful. Instead of celebrating people who are universally beautiful we should look at the unfortunate looking people and try to find beauty within them. There lies the true challenge and ultimately the key to self acceptance and happiness.
